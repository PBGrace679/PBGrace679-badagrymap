BADAGRY

Badagry is an ancient town that shares border with benin republic. A lot of development like education and Christianity came to Nigeria through Badagry.

Tribe Which Make Badagry

The Ogu people popularly called Egun people is the tribe found in Badagry.The prevalence of resources and water swamps resulted in the eating of crabs, fishes, lobsters, and snails.De Wheno Aholu Akran Menu Toyi I, the present Akran of Badagry, is the paramount ruler of Egun land
